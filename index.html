Check to make sure clients are happy

Here you can send a link to clients who have recently instructed the firm to ask if they are satisfied with the service they have received to date. Often clients can experience a dissatisfaction if their understanding of how their case will progress is not how things have started out. By sending a request early on, the client will have the opportunity to let you know if something is not quite right - without this being made public. Having an early understanding of the issues allows you the ability to take any corrective action. This service also gives you the peace of mind to know that everything is OK.
